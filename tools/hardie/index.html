Lap products overlap 1.25" minimum, so max exposure is plank width − 1.25". HardieShingle panels are 48" wide (7" max exposure straight edge, 6" staggered); HardiePanel sheets install with no exposure. Enter wall length × height or square feet directly — the total includes your waste factor.

Waste guide: 10% covers simple rectangular walls with few openings. Go 15% when gable ends, dormers, or lots of windows/doors force short angled cuts — gables waste the most near the peak, where drops are too short to reuse. Use 20%+ for multiple gables or complex rooflines.
